# Logoplacers — AI-readable product summary

> Logoplacers is a browser-based SaaS tool for B2B sales teams that enables visual personalisation of outbound email at scale.

## What it does

Logoplacers allows sales professionals (AEs, SDRs, BDRs) to:
- Upload a product screenshot or marketing image as a template
- Automatically fetch company logos for a list of prospects (by domain or company name)
- Place each prospect's logo on the template, pixel-perfectly positioned
- Generate personalised images for every prospect simultaneously (bulk)
- Send personalised emails with attached images directly from Gmail via Google OAuth
- Export personalised images as a ZIP file

## Who uses it
- SDRs and AEs doing cold email outreach
- Sales teams running personalised demo campaigns
- Revenue teams looking to improve reply rates on cold outreach

## Key differentiators
- Entirely browser-based: no backend, no data storage, all processing client-side
- Gmail integration via Google OAuth (send-only scope)
- Auto logo detection: Clearbit → Google Favicons → DuckDuckGo fallback chain
- Anti-spam delays built into bulk sending (15–45 second random delays)
- HEIC image support (auto-converted in browser)
- Video personalisation mode

## Pricing
- Free: 4 credits/day, single export
- SDR: $19/month, 300 credits/month, bulk send unlocked
- Sales Pro: $29/month, 2,000 credits/month (most popular)
- Team: $59/month, 10,000 credits/month, up to 5 seats
